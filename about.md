### The Vision: Why Engineering?
For many students in Pakistan, the letters “UET” represent more than just an acronym; they symbolize the pinnacle of technical excellence. My journey toward the University of Engineering and Technology began with a curiosity about how the world functions—from the mechanics of a simple pendulum to the complex flow of electrons in a circuit.

### The Grind: Mastering the Fundamentals
The preparation for the ECAT was an exercise in mental discipline. I spent months immersed in the complexities of Mathematics and Physics, tracing the behavior of limits in Multivariable Calculus and the elegance of Electrostatics.
